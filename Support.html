Support Us

The Green Coalition relies on community support to power our work for a healthier, more sustainable Hemet. Your donations help us lead grassroots movements against harmful warehouse developments, advocate for a green future, and increase access to resources for the community. With your help, we plant trees, host educational events, and engage in meaningful public outreach to protect our environment and uplift our home. Every contribution—big or small—helps us create lasting change. Thank you for standing with us. You can also help by joining us in our mission! Contact us at green.san.jacinto@gmail.com to see how you can get involved.
