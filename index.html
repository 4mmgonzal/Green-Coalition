Our Mission

To create a sustainable community for the San Jacinto Valley. A sustainable community is a community of residents and neighbors, who work together to balance ecological, economic, and social needs to ensure a clean, healthy, and safe environment for all members of society and for generations to come.

Support Our Mission

The Green Coalition relies on community support to power our work for a healthier, more sustainable San Jacinto. Your donations help us lead grassroots movements against harmful warehouse developments, advocate for a safer city, and increase access to resources for those in need. With your help, we can keep developing sustainably, hosting educational events, and engaging in meaningful public outreach to protect and uplift our community. Every contribution helps us create lasting change. Thank you for standing with us.
